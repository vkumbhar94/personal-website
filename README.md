### About Me

Hello! I'm Vaibhav Kumbhar, a software engineer with 9 years of experience building backend systems and distributed infrastructure, plus 2+ years leading engineering teams. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Walchand College of Engineering (2016).

I currently lead the Logs platform team at LogicMonitor as a Lead Software Engineer. Over my time there I've scaled the Argus monitoring platform to handle 10K+ resources and built its Kubernetes monitoring from the ground up, before moving on to architect the Logs platform. Earlier, at Veritas/Cohesity, I worked on a disaster recovery platform with AWS integration and Azure asset discovery. I'm based in Pune, India.

I'm passionate about building robust, high-scale distributed systems, mentoring engineers, and contributing to open source. I'm excited to connect and explore opportunities for growth and collaboration.

My technical skillset includes:

- **Distributed Systems**: Microservices, Kafka, event-driven architecture

- **Leadership**: Team leadership, mentorship, and technical strategy
